The problem with employee reimbursements

Current travel and purchase workflows push costs onto employees, fill inboxes with receipts, and create delays for everyone involved.

! Today
  • • Employees must front costs for employer approved travel and materials.
  • • They wait weeks or months for reimbursement, often across multiple claims.
  • • Admin teams are overloaded by receipt checking, resubmissions, and incomplete documentation.
  • • ERR and compliance rules slow everything down when formats and evidence are inconsistent.
  • • Universities and mid sized organisations often lack dedicated travel or procurement desks, so process responsibility is scattered.
  • • Everyone suffers from unnecessary delay and complexity built around the idea of reimbursement.

Reimbursement is the wrong default. The employer already approves the spend and carries the risk. The process still forces employees to carry the cash flow and admin.

Claimless removes reimbursement entirely and replaces it with a vendor based procurement flow. No employee should need a credit card to work.

Claimless: a procurement vendor instead of reimbursement

Claimless plugs directly into your existing approval processes and behaves like any other vendor. The difference is that it removes employees from the payment and reimbursement cycle entirely.

✓ How it works

Claimless sits alongside your existing finance and procurement policies. Managers and grant holders continue to approve spend as normal, but instead of asking employees to pay first and claim later, Claimless executes the spend as a vendor.

  • V Claimless registers as a procurement vendor with your organisation.
  • V Employees forward internal approvals and supplier quotes directly to Claimless.
  • V Claimless verifies approvals with finance or procurement and receives a Purchase Order (PO).
  • V Claimless handles all bookings, purchases, ERR compliant receipts, and invoice formatting.
  • V Employees never pay upfront. Employers receive clean documentation and a single vendor invoice.
From approval to execution
Vendor based workflow

Benefits for employees and employers

Moving from reimbursement to a procurement vendor model removes friction and risk while preserving existing approval structures.

For employees No more out of pocket
  • ✓ No out of pocket spending on flights, hotels, registration fees, or equipment.
  • ✓ No interest charges or credit card juggling while waiting for reimbursement.
  • ✓ No weeks or months spent tracking reimbursement claims and resubmissions.
  • ✓ Stress free booking and travel support from Claimless.
  • ✓ Clean documentation for any internal reporting or grant audits.
Travel becomes a normal work activity again instead of a personal cash flow problem.
For employers Operational gains
  • ✓ Reduced admin burden and fewer touchpoints in the reimbursement queue.
  • ✓ Far fewer receipt rejections or missing evidence, because Claimless controls the booking and documentation.
  • ✓ ERR compliance built into every receipt and invoice produced by Claimless.
  • ✓ Predictable invoicing via PO that fits within existing vendor payment cycles.
  • ✓ Happier, more productive staff who are not using personal credit to fund organisational travel.
Claimless works like a normal vendor in your system, but removes an entire class of reimbursement workload.
